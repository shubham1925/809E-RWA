RWA2 Instructions

NOTE:
1. Route taken may not be the shortest.
2. Reaching a goal may take lot of time.
3. Robot might pass too close to an obstacle without collision.

Run:
Terminal 1-
  export TURTLEBOT3_MODEL=burger
  roslaunch turtlebot3_gazebo turtlebot3_world.launch x_pos:=-2.0 y_pos:=0.0

Terminal 2:
  rosrun rwa2_sonawane my_bot_controller x (where x is a value in [0,5])
